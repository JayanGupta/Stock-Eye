# Stock-Eye: Warehouse Inventory Management System 

## Overview
Stock-Eye is a cutting-edge project designed to streamline warehouse inventory management using computer vision and machine learning. The system tracks items in the warehouse, predicts restocking needs based on future demand, and monitors monthly sales to identify the most sold items by category.

## Project Structure
- `data/`: Contains datasets for training and testing computer vision models, as well as sales data.
- `models/`: Machine learning models for inventory tracking and demand forecasting.
- `scripts/`: Python scripts for data preprocessing, model training, evaluation, and prediction.
- `results/`: Evaluation metrics, sales reports, and visualizations of inventory and sales data.
- `vision/`: Computer vision algorithms and models for item tracking in the warehouse.

## Features
- **Inventory Tracking**: Uses computer vision to monitor and manage the quantity and location of items in the warehouse.
- **Restocking Prediction**: Machine learning models predict future restocking needs based on historical sales data and trends.
- **Sales Monitoring**: Tracks monthly sales and highlights the most sold items by category to aid in inventory management and decision-making.

## Results
The Stock-Eye project provides several key insights:
- **Restocking Predictions**: Accurate forecasts of future inventory needs based on historical sales and trends.
- **Sales Reports**: Detailed monthly sales data and visualizations identifying the most sold items by category.
- **Inventory Tracking Accuracy**: Metrics and visualizations demonstrating the performance of the computer vision models in tracking warehouse items.
